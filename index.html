Data and Voice

The core of your high speed business.

Whether you’re making a phone call, accessing a database, sharing a file, viewing a training video, closing a transaction, or sending an email, you’re doing it over a network. Networks are the indispensible foundation for delivery of advanced IT services and complex mission-critical applications such as customer resource management (CRM), supply-chain management (SCM), or transactional applications.

Virtually all organizations depend on networks to connect users with IT services, anytime, anywhere. IntelliDwellingdesigns and deploys flexible, reliable, secure networks—on time and within budget. We are experts in routing, switching, wireless, network security, and optimization. We also know how to supplement your networks with standards-based products to provide the exact capabilities you need to operate and manage your network.

Network Services We Offer:

  • Data center and virtualization
  • High-performance switching
  • Optical networking
  • Storage networking,
  • Server and application networking
  • Borderless networks—Wired and wireless service delivery to users anytime, anywhere: routers, switches, WAN optimization, optical and storage networking, network management and automation
  • Collaboration—Networks supporting communications services such as voice calls, messaging, call centers, audio conferences, online meetings, and teleconferencing

CCTV/IP Cameras

The cornerstone of any security system.

IntelliDwelling offers a wide range of CCTV security cameras; from multi-camera, all-weather options, to covert video surveillance devices. No matter what your need, there's a solution available for you to monitor your office, store or facility. Protect your property from vandalism and theft with a CCTV or IP camera system. Our surveillance camera systems allow you to monitor your site from one central location. Protect what matters most with a video surveillance system from IntelliDwelling.

IntelliDwelling will design and install everything from systems consisting of one to two cameras to systems that need to provide surveillance in multiple facilities, or segments of a building, with digital recording. IntelliDwelling systems provide our customers with the ability to access individual cameras or recordings from one central location or security office via the local network or the Internet.

What can Intellidwelling do for you?

  • CCTV Systems Design, Engineering, and Installation
  • Digital Recorder Systems (accessible from any device).
  • Conventional Multiplexer and VCR Systems
  • Camera Installation (wide variety to fit any system)
  • Hidden Camera Installation
  • System Integration (tie-in with security, access control, building automation)
  • Annual Test and Inspection, Preventative Maintenance, and Repair Service programs are available.

Access Control

Control who goes where.

Whether you need to secure one point of entry, or have a facility that will involve hundreds of doors on several floors, or even in multiple buildings, IntelliDwelling can engineer and design a system to meet your needs. With an IntelliDwelling Access Control system, you can use any web-enabled computer or compatible device to:

  • Monitor all sensitive areas of your facility
  • Control employee access
  • Stay informed and grant real-time access to clients and guests with video and intercom entry systems

  • Card-reader Systems
  • Biometric Readers
  • Keypad Systems
  • Stand-alone Systems (single door)
  • Elevator Control
  • Installation of Electronic Door Hardware (strikes, electromagnetic locks, electrified latches or rods)
  • Annual Test and Inpsection, Preventative Maintenance, and Repair Service programs are also available.

Security and Alamrm systems

Custom alarm solutions for any dwelling.

Whether you are a small business or the largest commercial enterprise, IntelliDwelling has the right security solution for you. Our professionals have the expertise needed to deliver customized, intelligent solutions that meet your unique challenges, improve operational effectiveness, increase cost efficiencies and achieve a higher level of safety and security for your organization. IntelliDwelling understands that each of our customers businesses and facilities are unique. Your security system will be custom designed to meet the needs of your facility and type of business. Whether your system will consist of a few doors and motion detectors, or your facility will have multiple buildings, floors, or tenants, we can design and monitor the appropriate security system for you.

  • Security System Design
  • Installation
  • Control Panels
  • Keypads
  • Perimeter Protection Including Doors and Windows
  • Motion Detection
  • Glass-break Detection
  • Panic and Hold-Up Alarms
  • Environmental ProtectionExamples:Gas and Carbon Monoxide Detection
  • High Water Detection
  • Boiler Temperature Supervision
  • Cool and Freezer Supervision
  • AC Power Loss (phase monitors)
  • High and Low Room Temperature Supervision
